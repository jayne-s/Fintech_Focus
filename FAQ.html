Frequently Asked Questions

How can I drink more water?

Try drinking a glass upon waking and one with every meal. For a flavor boost, add a spritz of citrus, like lemon or lime. Proper hydration is vital for health. It helps regulate body temperature, removes waste, lubricates joints, helps prevent infections, shuttles nutrients to cells and helps organs function properly. Aim to drink 7-8 glasses of water every day.

What is BMI?

Body mass index, or BMI, is a measure of body size. It combines a person’s weight with their height. The results of a BMI measurement can give an idea about whether a person has the correct weight for their height.

BMI Catgories: Underweight = <18.5 | Normal weight = 18.5–24.9 | Overweight = 25–29.9 | Obesity = BMI of 30 or greater.

How many hours should I exercise?

Get at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous aerobic activity a week, or a combination of moderate and vigorous activity. To provide even greater health benefits, at least 300 minutes a week is recommended. Do strength training exercises, around 12-15 repetitions, for all major muscle groups at least two times a week.

What are the symptoms of mental health disorders?

Mental illness disorders include anxiety, bipolar disorder, depression, obsessive compulsive disorder, post-traumatic stress disorder, eating disorders and many others. The most common symptoms of mental health disorders include changes in feelings and demeanor, loss of interest, change in sleeping habits, low energy, difficulty interacting with others, uncontrollable emotions, and appetite/weight changes.

How many hours should I sleep?

The National Sleep Foundation guidelines advise that healthy adults need between 7 and 9 hours of sleep per night. Babies, young children, and teens need even more sleep to enable their growth and development. People over 65 should also get 7 to 8 hours per night. Stick to the same sleep schedule every day, even on weekends. Try to minimize light and sound distractions, and disconnect from electronic devices at least 30 minutes before your bed time.
